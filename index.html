Why NEXUS Corporation

NEXUS Corporation is one of the best leading association of companies with wide experience in Infrastructure, Architecture, Land external development, High rise building, Civil, Electrical & Mechanical works with quality. We provide you complete and diversified solution to your challenges and problems. We also have specialized expertise in renewable energy as well as lighting system. We carry out all aspects of execution for all construction projects with modernized approach, from concept to creation.

NEXUS engineering consulting services incorporate research and development in designing. It provides modernized construction and engineering solutions that conform to contractual & regulatory requirements. Believe in teamwork to improve the quality and strive for the highest standards of ethical behaviour, commitments and dedication that serve as core values.

Vision

Our vision is to provide most efficient EPC construction services in all over the Pakistan and to become a recognized and trusted entity in all types of construction services from concept to its creation. With our vast experience and the collective knowledge of the professionals and experts in our organization we provide multiple beneﬁts to our clients which cover all aspects of construction phases.

Being a consultant, we have a vision to build a long term and sustainable relation with our client. We aim to provide least cost acceptable modernized design with forward thinking and management services. Research and development essentially incorporated in our consultancy services to fulfil current demand and upcoming future needs of the project and societies. Similarly, like construction services we provide all types of engineering consultancy under one roof.

Mission

Our intension is to be a reliable solution provider for our valued clients. Our mission is to provide quality Engineering, Procurement, construction, consultancy, supervision throughout the region with highest level of professionalism and honesty in our relationships with our clients, employees, vendors, and other stake holders. Moreover, it expands new vision of development in service provider companies that build on our competencies, experiences, and knowledge to deliver more than our client’s expectations.

Director's Message

After serving at key posts of various organization in the last decade, I concluded that there is a considerable vacuum in the field of engineering consultancy/construction in govt as well as private sector in Pakistan and across the globe as well. The purpose of inception of Nexus Corporation is to fill that vacuum and therefore the main focus of Nexus as a solution provider is to offer multiple solutions to client requirement within their financial resources, space limitations, time constraints and design requirements. Our designs are harmonized with the universal pattern and cultural orientation while keeping in view the connective logics and usage concept of our client.

I strongly believe that NEXUS Corporation Pakistan is a type and level of consultancy/construction/solution provider which will bring competition among its market competitors to provide an environment where questioning and reasoning shall be the binding force between client and the solution provider. The innovation, dynamism and ethicalism are the core values of Nexus Corporation Pakistan.

QHSE (Quality, Health, Safety & Environmental)

Nexus Corporation strives to achieve the highest standards in the areas of Quality, Health, Safety and Environment across all its operations. Our commitment within each of these areas ensures best practice of industry for QHSE and sets the bar for our competition.
